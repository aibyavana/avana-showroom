01 / The Number That Matters

Start here.

Before the full breakdown — the single finding that pays for fixing everything else. This is what was leaving the account every year while the store looked like it was working.

Estimated Revenue Lost · Abandoned Checkouts · 2025
~$1.05M / year

67.6% of checkout sessions were abandoned — 2,334 of 3,453 sessions that reached checkout never completed. At their 2025 average order value of ~$451, that's roughly a million dollars in revenue that reached the doorstep and walked away. A correctly sequenced recovery flow typically recaptures 5-15% of abandoned checkouts. Even a conservative recovery rate turns this into six figures recaptured annually — which is why it's the first thing Ava fixes.

What Ava reads across the ledger
Two leaks dominate and they compound: a funnel that converts at 0.37% means most traffic never reaches checkout — and then two-thirds of the few who do, leave. Fixing checkout recovery recaptures money already at the door; fixing the funnel widens the door. The honest read is that returns are fine and paid is a measurement problem — naming what isn't broken is what makes the rest credible.
